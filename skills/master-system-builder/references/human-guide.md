# Human guide

## What the system is

An expert package is a private, evidence-backed research system. It is not the real person, a guarantee of correctness, or permission to redistribute their work.

The human controls access, purpose, privacy, and final judgment. The AI handles inventory, normalization, evidence mapping, retrieval, and repeatable analysis.

## Useful requests

```text
Answer using only primary evidence in this expert package. Cite source files and timestamps. Mark anything else as inference or unknown.
```

```text
Use the <framework-id> framework to review this proposal. Separate matches, violations, tradeoffs, and missing evidence.
```

```text
Do not imitate the expert's voice. Apply only the expert's method to rewrite this email in my brand voice.
```

```text
Compare expert A and expert B on this decision. Preserve disagreement and evidence boundaries.
```

```text
This is new authorized source material. Register it and create a distillate first. Do not change the soul until you show what evidence would justify the change.
```

## Add new material

Tell the agent:

- where the material came from;
- whether it is public, private, or paid;
- what you are authorized to do with it;
- who is speaking;
- what decision or topic it may improve.

Let the agent inventory and test one item before a large batch.

## Correct errors

Report the exact claim, the current source anchor, and the better evidence. Ask the agent to update the claim register, affected framework, index, and log without deleting the prior evidence trail.

## Know when to re-distill

Re-distill when new material introduces a new method, repeatedly strengthens a belief, changes a prior position, resolves an open question, or reveals an important boundary. A single promotional email usually should not rewrite the expert profile.
