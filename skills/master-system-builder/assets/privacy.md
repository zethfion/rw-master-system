# Privacy and release boundary

This package is private and user-controlled by default.

- Keep source material, distillates, and consultation logs out of public repositories.
- Keep credentials, cookies, tokens, signed URLs, personal data, and paid material out of version control.
- Do not change `public_release_allowed` to `true` until a human has reviewed a sanitized copy and explicitly approved publication.
- Run release-level validation before publishing any package or archive.
